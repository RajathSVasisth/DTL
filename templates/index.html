YOLO ASISTO!

Welcome to Airport Security Image Scanning for Threat Observation (ASISTO) centre.

We have used YOLO (You Only Look Once) darkflow to train our model. YOLO is a state-of-the-art, real-time object detection system where in a single neural network is applied to the full image. This network divides the image into regions and predicts bounding boxes and probabilities for each region. These bounding boxes are weighted by the predicted probabilities. Also we have used large Airport X-Ray datasets to train our model and thus offer promising results. SignUp to create an account and observe threats in Airport Scanning Images by just selecting the image from your device. Leave the rest to us!
